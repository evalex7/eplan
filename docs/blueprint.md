# **App Name**: AirAssist

## Core Features:

- Contract Management Table: Display a table with service contracts, maintenance dates, service types, and assigned engineers.
- Gantt Chart: Visualize the maintenance schedule using a Gantt chart for timeline planning.
- Kanban Board: Organize tasks and object assignments using a Kanban board, categorized by service engineer.
- Date Adjustment Assistant: AI tool to proactively analyze and suggest optimal rescheduling, with reasoning. The LLM evaluates service history, engineer availability, and equipment requirements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky blue (#72BCD4) to reflect the essence of air and cleanliness in climate control services.
- Background color: Light grayish-blue (#E0E5E7), creating a subtle and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#4A767D) for highlights, calls to action, and important information, providing a contrasting and easily noticeable element.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, to ensure a blend of modern appeal and legibility, fostering a welcoming atmosphere.
- Use industry-related icons to visually represent different maintenance tasks and equipment.
- Maintain a clean, card-based layout for easy navigation and information overview.
- Incorporate subtle transition animations to enhance user experience when navigating between tabs or updating task status.
